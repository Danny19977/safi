Specialty Coffee Beans

At Café SAFI PETIT KWILU, we source only the finest Congolese Arabica and Robusta beans—handpicked from the fertile hills of Kwilu and Kivu. Each batch is carefully sun-dried and expertly roasted to unlock deep, complex flavors. Whether you prefer bright citrus notes or smoky chocolate richness, our beans deliver an unforgettable cup. Perfect for home brewers and coffee connoisseurs alike..

Signature Coffee Bags

Take the taste of Congo home with our freshly packed, airtight coffee bags—available in 250g, 500g, and 1kg sizes. Every bag is filled with our premium beans, ground or whole, to suit your brewing style. Designed for freshness, our resealable packaging ensures your coffee stays aromatic and flavorful for weeks. Ideal for gifting or your daily ritual.

Premium Packaging & Gifts

Elevate your coffee experience with our elegant, reusable tins and handcrafted gift boxes, featuring vibrant Congolese designs. Perfect for corporate gifts, special occasions, or treating yourself to something extraordinary. Each set includes our signature blend, a personalized note, and brewing tips. Because great coffee deserves beautiful presentation.
