SAFIA GROUP: Our Profile

SAFIA GROUP is an innovative agricultural company dedicated to the coffee industry. We specialize in PETIT KWILU, a coffee variety truly unique to the world, cultivated exclusively in Kongo Central, DRC. Founded in 2020 by Regine Tika Shungu, our main goal is to promote Congolese coffee, especially the Petit Kwilu, and establish the DRC as a key player in the international coffee market.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to promote and add value to high-quality Congolese coffee. We support local producers, create sustainable jobs, and aim to make Petit Kwilu a globally recognized brand.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leader in the PETIT KWILU coffee industry, both in the DRC and internationally. We're doing this by developing the brand, creating a chain of Congolese coffee shops, and contributing to the sustainable economic development of the country.

Core Activities

  • Growing and producing PETIT KWILU coffee in Kongo Central.
  • Processing: roasting and packaging to ensure top quality.
  • Selling locally and exporting worldwide.
  • Developing a chain of modern coffee shops.
  • Partnering with businesses like hotels, restaurants, supermarkets, and other key players.
  • Training producers and raising awareness about coffee quality.

Services We Offer

  • Selling coffee beans, sachets, and premium packaged coffee.
  • Providing technical assistance and training to improve production quality.
  • Managing and developing our coffee shops.
  • Exporting coffee to Europe and other markets.
  • Forging B2B partnerships with hotels, restaurants, and distributors.
  • Promoting Congolese coffee, particularly #PETITKWILU, through events and campaigns.

About Us

Founded in 2020 by Regine Tika Shungu, SAFIA GROUP promotes the DRC's globally unique PETIT KWILU coffee, grown exclusively in Kongo Central, to the international market.
